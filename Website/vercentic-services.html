Add-on services

Modular support.

Any of the following can be added to any tier, or engaged independently. All scoped individually.

Data migration
Extraction, cleansing, mapping, and validated import from legacy ATS, spreadsheets, or third-party systems. Includes reconciliation report.
Integration build
Custom connectors for HRIS, assessment platforms, job boards, video interview tools, or internal systems.
Portal design
Branded career site, hiring manager portal, or agency portal — fully configured and styled. Includes copywriting for standard sections.
Custom reporting
Bespoke dashboards and reports against your KPIs. Includes metric definition, build, and training.
AI configuration
Copilot prompt tuning, job description templates, screening questionnaire design, and matching model calibration.
Training delivery
Role-specific sessions — recruiter, hiring manager, admin. Delivered live, remotely. Includes materials and recordings.
Health check
Structured review of a live environment — configuration quality, adoption metrics, AI performance, and optimisation recommendations.
Interim admin
A Vercentic-trained administrator works in your environment on a short-term basis — configuration, training, and system management.
What to expect

Our commitments.

We run a small number of engagements at any time so every client gets the attention the project deserves.

A named point of contact
Every engagement has a named Vercentic consultant as your primary contact throughout — not a helpdesk, not a rotating team. They will know your configuration, your team, and your goals.
Honest scoping
We will tell you if something is out of scope, if a timeline is unrealistic, or if a requirement needs rethinking. A smooth go-live matters more to us than winning additional scope.
Documented configuration
Every decision made during implementation is documented. You receive a full configuration record at go-live — what was built, why, and how to maintain it independently.
Capability, not dependency
The goal of every engagement is a team that can run the platform independently. We track this actively and do not consider implementation complete until it is met.
Transparent progress
Weekly status updates, a shared project tracker, and risks flagged as they appear. You should never be asking where things are.
